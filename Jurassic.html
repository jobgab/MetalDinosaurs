Wait so... the Jurassic was also Metal??

Heck YEAH it was!! And I'll tell you why, but first, you know what I find Ironic? That some of the most iconic dinosaurs that appear in Jurassic Park, actually weren't from the Jurassic period. Like the T-rex! The T-rex was still far from existing during the Jurassic (its reign was actually during the Cretaceous). Still, the Jurassic had some pretty metal dinosaurs, like the Stegosaurus, or the Diplodocus.

By the way, did you know that Diplodocus, which is a sauropod (those loooong necked dinosaurs) could potentially break the barrier of sound using it's tail as a whip?? That's pretty metal, in my opinion. After all, can YOU break the barrier of sound with your tail?? Because I can't, I don't even have a tail. So, I would actually be pretty impressed if you could. That'd be pretty metal for sure.
